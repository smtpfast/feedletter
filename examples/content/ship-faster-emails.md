Transactional email is infrastructure, but it still needs product thinking.
Start with domain authentication, clear templates, plain-text fallbacks, and
delivery logs your support team can inspect.

Teams usually wait too long to add bounce handling and suppression lists. Add
them before your first meaningful launch so bad addresses do not become a
reputation problem.
